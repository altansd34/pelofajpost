    America’s Got Talent‘s Simon Cowell is keen on splashing his cash on a brand new holiday home in Barbados. The TV judge is a regular to the island, spending his entire Christmas break on it’s beautiful coastline.

    Simon Cowell planning to buy a $10 million house in Barbados

    It’s no secret that Simon Cowell has earned himself a hefty amount of cash in his years working in both the TV and the music industry. And, with a large collection of sports cars and three luxury homes, the mogul is set to make his next big investment.

    According to a source, who spoke to The Sun, “Simon loves Barbados and is interested in laying down roots there. He would like to splash up to £10 million on a pad, with a beachfront view and plenty of guest rooms.”

    ‘He knows a lot of the locals now so feels right at home and relaxed when he’s on the island,” the source continued.

    Simon Cowell has spent many years on the island during the Christmas months, enjoying it bright sunshine and clear water beaches as a getaway from a hectic life in London and Los Angeles.

    Where are Simon’s other homes?

    Cowell splits his time between Los Angeles and London. The TV executive has two homes in exclusive areas of London, and a large villa in Malibu. The AGT judge has mentioned that he has quick access between both cities, as he’s now equipped with a private jet on hand at all times.

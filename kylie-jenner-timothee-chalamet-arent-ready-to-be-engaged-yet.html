    Kylie Jenner and Timothee Chalamet may be serious in their present romance, but they’re not necessarily ready to tie the knot anytime soon. A source recently revealed that the Dune star is allegedly looking for a Cartier engagement ring.

    Kylie Jenner, Timothee Chalamet are “Madly In Love” With Each Other

    Jenner and Chalamet have been dating for about one year now, and their romance is reportedly not anything casual. This fact may seem obvious, given their unapologetic public display of affection during the 2024 Golden Globes.

    “Timothée and Kylie are madly in love,” one source said.

    Since news of their blooming relationship broke, multiple sources have given updates about them. Apparently, an insider reported that Chalamet was “recently browsing Cartier engagement rings.” However, another source keenly denied the claim, stating that the Wonka star is not shopping for any engagement ring.

    Hence, with all the reports released, it’s safe to assume that the actor doesn’t have any plans on proposing yet. Be that as it may, the pair’s relationship is clearly nothing less than casual, as Chalamet has already met Jenner’s kids — five-year-old Stormi and 23-month-old Aire, although they don’t know that he’s their mommy’s boyfriend.

    “Timothée has met them in group situations where there were other family members or friends around,” another insider said. “It hasn’t been anything where it’s just Kylie, Timothée, and the kids in an intimate setting. And she introduced him as one of Mommy’s friends. Kylie feels like it’s [still] too soon to let them know anything about their relationship.”

    Jenner and Chalamet began getting to know each other in early 2023. By the end of the year, the two spent a “good deal” of time together during the holidays. Chalamet attended the Kardashians’ Christmas Eve party.

    It’s no secret that not all fans have been supportive of the couple’s relationship. Be that as it may, “everything is going great between them,” according to one source.

    Travis Scott is Reportedly Bitter About His Ex’s New Romance

    While Chalamet and Jenner’s relationship have been going strong, the Kylie Cosmetics’ CEO’s ex-lover Traviss Scott is reportedly sour about them. According to an unknown source, the rapper was reportedly left “agitated” after he saw them together as he’s doing “his best to avoid” memories with his ex.

    “Travis is agitated anytime he sees Kylie with him,” an insider told DailyMail. “His feelings about them together are known already and he has addressed it in his music.”

    Reportedly, Scott believes that Jenner has “downgraded.” Furthermore, the rapper thinks that “she is trying to get to him.” In turn, the “SICKO MODE” performer is attempting to “block out all the other noise.”

    “He knows him and Kylie are done for good and does not want to be around the two of them together,” the source said. “He is actively involved with his kids and always will be there for them.”

    However, despite the bitter feeling, Scott has reportedly moved on. According to a source, he has “no shortage of women to choose from.”

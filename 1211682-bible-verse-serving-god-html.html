    Bible verse about serving God with joy will help you understand christian service better. People believe that every Christian is called to bear responsibility for his personal spiritual growth, as well as service in his family, local church, and society. Also, the New Testament states that every Christian is endowed with spiritual gifts from God. These gifts determine his place with the Body of Christ (the Church) and are unique tools for serving others.

    So learn these Bible verse in serving God and find out what you can do!

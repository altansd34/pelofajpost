    A birthday is usually a great time for one to celebrate the life and grace God has given them. When a friend or family member's birthday comes, it is nice to celebrate them by sending some thoughtful messages, quotes, and prayers. At times, coming up with the perfect message is easier said than done, which is why we have come up with a collection of amazing birthday prayers and messages to send your loved ones. Nelson Iheagwam, lead pastor at The Equipping Center Global in the United kingdom, explained the need for these prayers for our loved ones.

    When a person turns a year older, encourage them by reminding them of God's unending love and presence in their lives. Whether the person in question is a family member, a colleague, or a friend, these messages will make them smile on their special day.

    • May all of your hopes, dreams, and aspirations come true for you as you begin this new year of your life. You deserve all of the very best things to come your way, my dear boy.

    Happy New Year prayer for your partner, family and friends

    In a conversation with Nelson Iheagwam, lead pastor at The Equipping Center Global, he shared with Legit.ng the kind of birthday wishes and prayers he offers his wife:

    My prayer for my wife on her birthdays is that she fulfills God's purpose for her life, lives in alignment with His plan, and doesn't feel limited by our marriage when looking back in her old age.I also wish her the blessings of 3 John 1:2, "Beloved, I wish above all things that thou mayest prosper and be in health, even as thy soul prospereth." This means I pray for her prosperity in all aspects, not just material and emotional but also in her spiritual journey and relationship with God.

    Pastor Nelson, in his opinion, said this is the most significant prayer for one's spouse because everyone will be held accountable for their lives on the day of judgement as written in Romans 14:12.

    Watching a movie while covered in a fluffy blanket and eating your favorite treats is such a warm, cozy feeling that it’s almost indescribable. And the fact is that sometimes you need to take some time for yourself and allow yourself to do something just because you want to — without having to worry too much about whether or not you’ll gain weight from it or what people will think of you. It’s a moment of peace that deserves to be enjoyed.

    For this reason, the Bright Side team has decided to pull some movies that accompanied you in those moments out of our trunk of memories, and we want to show you how 18 of your favorite actresses look today. Take a peek!

    Strolling along the streets of Venice, Italy, supermodel Heidi Klum and musician Tom Kaulitz are clearly enjoying their vacation. Apparently though, some stolen photos have made fans speculate that the AGT judge might be expecting a fifth child.

    Fans Suspect that Heidi Klum is Pregnant

    Earlier this week, the visually pleasing pair were seen walking around affectionately in the Floating City. Klum and Kaulitz, like their usual selves, were definitely unafraid of showing off their love for each other. Nonetheless, fans particularly noticed how the couple cannot seem to take their eyes off the German beauty’s stomach.

    Many times amid strolling, Kaulitz placed his hand on Klum’s stomach while looking at it lovingly. The musician’s noticeable gaze is seemingly ignited by Klum’s body language as well. In one stolen snap, Klum is resting her hand on her tummy. During the same afternoon, Klum was also stopping to take selfies with fans.

    For their break, Klum was wearing a floor-length black lace skirt which she paired with a black bralette top. She then completed her ensemble with a slouchy leather purse in buff a pair of thong sandals. Kaulitz, on the other hand, wore a tangerine button-down shirt, khaki linen pants, brown shoes and retro-style sunglasses.

    “I mean, I don’t know anybody who does this, unless they are expecting … Janet Jackson gave birth at 50 years old… and Heidi is happily married. It’s plausible,” one user tweeted.

    While their afternoon walk gestures may possibly suggest a fifth child coming, the same may also mean nothing. Nonetheless, it’s indeed adorable to see the couple being sweet and free in public.

    She Recently Posted a Series of Sultry Bikini Snaps on Instagram

    On Instagram, Klum generously uploaded slideshows of their vacation. The supermodel casually showed off her beach body in photos where she is wearing a sexy two-piece bikini.

    “Vacanza romantico,” she wrote. 

    In the post, Klum also shared a selfie with her husband as they were riding a boat. In another post, the AGT judge shared a clip where she was slightly dancing as she was enjoying the majestic view of the ocean.

    Last week, the couple went for a Gondola ride. For the said outing, Klum wore a a black dress while Kaulitz sported a white linen shirt. To elevate their aesthetic, the couple then carried a black lacey umbrella.

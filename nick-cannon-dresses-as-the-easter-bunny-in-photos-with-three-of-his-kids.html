    Nick Cannon recently got into the Easter spirit while spending time with three of his kids. The Masked Singer host dressed up like the Easter Bunny to pose with Zion, Zillion, and Beautiful, whom he shares with Abby De La Rosa.

    Nick Cannon Dresses as the Easter Bunny in New Photos

    In a series of Instagram photos, Cannon can be seen wearing a fuzzy white bunny costume and posing in front of an inflatable rainbow and Easter eggs with his kids, who are wearing bunny ears. In some shots, Cannon has his face showing, while in others, he’s wearing a bunny head.

    The kids also posed with their mom, Abby De La Rosa, for a few shots. The Instagram posts also include a shot of Cannon playing basketball with the kids while still wearing the bunny costume. Another photo shows him looking exhausted as he lies on the bench. The photos are captioned, “Life is Eggcelent.”

    Cannon and De La Rosa welcomed twin boys Zion and Zillion in 2021. Then last year, the pair welcomed a baby daughter named Beautiful Zeppelin. In total, Cannon has welcomed 12 children with six different women over the years.

    Cannon Compared Himself to Santa Claus at Christmas

    This isn’t Cannon’s first time associating himself with a holiday figure. In December, Cannon compared himself to Santa Claus as he traveled to spend time with his many children for Christmas.

    “Me and Santa do it together … I have the same vibe as Santa Claus. I’m traveling all night,” Cannon joked as he volunteered at the L.A. Mission alongside Brittany Bell and their kids Golden and Powerful Queen.

    Seeing Cannon in a bunny costume makes us think of his show The Masked Singer. The host has actually appeared in a costume on the show before. In Season 5, he appeared as Bulldog while Niecy Nash was guest-hosting.

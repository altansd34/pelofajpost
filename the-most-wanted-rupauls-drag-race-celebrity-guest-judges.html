    RuPaul’s Drag Race has become a cultural phenomenon, captivating audiences with its vibrant and colorful drag performances for years. While the show has had many incredible guest judges over the years, there are still many celebrities who we would love to see on the judging panel. We have compiled a dream guest judge list of celebrities that we believe would bring their unique perspectives and energy to the show.

    Celebrities We’d Love to See As a Guest Judge on RuPaul’s Drag Race

    1. Billy Porter

    A celebrity who would make a great addition to the judging panel is Billy Porter. Porter has become a trailblazer for gender fluidity and has used his platform to advocate for LGBTQ+ rights. His experience as a Broadway performer and fashion icon would make him an ideal candidate for a guest judge spot on the show.

    2. Trixie Mattel

    We would also love to see drag queen and recording artist Trixie Mattel as a guest judge on the show. Mattel has already made appearances on the show as a contestant and as a host of its spin-off series. Her wit, humor, and musical talent would make her a great addition to the judging panel.

    3. Tiffany Haddish

    Tiffany Haddish would be an excellent choice for a guest judge. Haddish has made a name for herself as one of the funniest and most relatable comedians working today. Her quick wit and infectious personality would bring a new level of humor and entertainment to the show.

    4. Laverne Cox

    As an actress and advocate for transgender rights, Laverne Cox would be a perfect fit for a guest judge spot. She has been vocal about her love of the show. She has even appeared as a guest host on its spin-off series. Her experience in the entertainment industry and passion for LGBTQ+ activism would make her a valuable addition to the judging panel.

    5. Dolly Parton

    With her iconic style and personality, Dolly Parton would be a dream guest judge for RuPaul’s Drag Race. She has been a vocal ally for the LGBTQ+ community and has worked with numerous drag performers over the years. Her experience as a country music legend and her larger-than-life persona would add a new level of fun and excitement to the show. There actually was an entire runway themed after the artist in Drag Race All Stars 7.

    6. Cardi B

    With her bold fashion choices and larger-than-life personality, Cardi B would be an excellent addition to the judging panel on RuPaul’s Drag Race. She has been an advocate for LGBTQ+ rights and has even performed in drag herself. Her experience as a musician and performer would make her a valuable guest judge, and her outspoken nature would add an extra level of entertainment to the show.

    7. Bruno Tonioli

    Dancing With the Stars and Britain’s Got Talent judge Bruno Tonioli would be an amazing addition to the Drag Race judging panel. He would be perfect to judge dance, since he’s a professionally trained choreographer. As loved on DWTS and BGT, Tonioli would not be able to sit still on this panel.

    8. Cher

    Fans are eagerly awaiting the day when Cher accepts the invite to be a guest judge. Ironically, Cher’s mother, Georgia Holt, and son, Chaz Bono, have already been on the show’s judging panel. In a publicly released statement, Cher has revealed that she’d join the show eventually, but it hasn’t happened yet.

    Demi Lovato is spreading holiday cheer this year with a festive treat for fans. The 31-year-old singer announced the arrival of A Very Demi Holiday Special, set to premiere on the Roku Channel on December 8. You can catch all the jolly tunes and seasonal joy through any Roku device.

    Demi Lovato Announces New Christmas Special For The Roku Channel

    In this upcoming program, Lovato will be serenading fans with both hit songs and classic holiday favorites. Get ready to sing along to timeless tunes like “Have Yourself a Merry Little Christmas,” “Rockin’ Around the Christmas Tree,” and “Jingle Bell Rock.” It’s a musical celebration that’s sure to put everyone in the Christmas spirit.

    The special promises more surprises with additional guest performers to be announced later. The anticipation builds as Lovato teases a “very special surprise duet” that will grace the stage alongside her. The singer is also serving as an executive producer for A Very Demi Holiday Special, alongside OBB Pictures. The holiday season just got a whole lot more exciting.

    “The holidays are such a special time of year, and I can’t wait to put my special twist on the celebrations!” Lovato said.

    In addition to this holiday special, Lovato has been keeping busy. They are currently working on their directorial debut in collaboration with OBB Pictures. Furthermore, next year, fans can look forward to the release of Child Star on Hulu, a documentary exploring the experiences of former child stars who grew up in the spotlight.

    Demi Lovato for “A Very Demi Holiday Special”, premiering December 8th on Roku. pic.twitter.com/vOEHt7WXY6

    Kicking off the New Year with a bang, Lovato takes the stage at The Chelsea at The Cosmopolitan of Las Vegas on December 31. This electrifying show promises to be the ultimate way to welcome 2024, with the performance set to start at 10:30 p.m., ensuring an unforgettable night of music and celebration.

    Tickets for this highly-anticipated event will be available to the general public starting Friday, October 6, at 10 a.m. PT via Ticketmaster. Fresh off a show-stopping performance at the 2023 MTV Video Music Awards and the release of her album Revamped, Lovato’s career is certainly on an exciting trajectory.

    As an advocate for mental health and with a massive global following, she continues to inspire and entertain audiences worldwide. Don’t miss your chance to start the New Year with Lovato in Las Vegas, a night filled with music, celebration, and memories to last a lifetime.

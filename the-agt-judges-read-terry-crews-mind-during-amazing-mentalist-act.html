    Kevin Micoud is more than just a sexy mentalist looking for his big break on AGT. He performed some Jedi mind tricks on the judges and Terry Crews, unlike anything we have ever seen before. This AGT hopeful definitely brought the wow factor during his unforgettable audition.

    Kevin Micoud Helps the ‘AGT’ Judges Read Terry Crews’ Mind

    Kevin announced that he would be performing mind transmission between the judges and Crews. Sofia Vergara and Heidi Klum each had to choose a photo of a past AGT finalist. Kevin extracted the mind of Crews. He grabbed the judge’s thoughts and put them inside of Crews’ head. This was demonstrated by the use of holograms.

    Crews closed his eyes and imagined his mind being taken from his head. Kevin held this orb that lit up and it was magic right before our eyes. Crews was instructed to imagine his mind coming back into his head. He was given a board to write the name of the two contestants that Vergara and Klum had pictures of. He wrote Kodi Lee and Shin Lim. 

    It turns out that those were the exact contestants that Vergara and Klum had photos of. At the very beginning of the act, Kevin left a photo of himself on the judge’s table. The photo contains his prediction which was also correct. Howie Mandel and Simon Cowell gave him a standing ovation. 

    “This act was sensational,” Cowell said. “It was taking this kind of act into the future. It was unlike anything we’ve ever seen before. This was a Vegas act, absolutely brilliant.”

    Mandel hilariously shouted about seeing Crews’ brain right before his eyes. The judges voted Kevin through to the next round of the competition. It will be so awesome to see how he tops his already stellar act if he makes it to the live shows.

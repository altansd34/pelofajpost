    Dancing With The Stars fans have been waiting eagerly for any kind of relationship developments between former dance partners Daniel Durant and Britt Stewart. The duo has sparked dating rumors time and time again, but Durant has reportedly confirmed they are dating in a recent Q&A.

    Daniel Durant, Britt Stewart Are Officially Dating

    During one of the stops of the DWTS live tour on Saturday, February 11, 2023, the group of performers took time to answer some of the most asked questions for them. Fans present at the Q&A claim that Durant revealed that he and his partner, Stewart are dating when he responded to a question about which cast members are in relationships.

    A short video showing Durant signing his answer to the crowd was met with widespread attention from fans. It was first posted on Instagram but is now currently available for viewing on a repost on Reddit.

    “Oh, I wanted to go back to the other question when you asked if anyone was dating,” Durant signed.

    The video then abruptly ends but fans in attendance revealed he talked about having taken the relationship with Stewart to the next level. They revealed that they are indeed currently dating and one fan claimed Durant signed they’re planning an announcement soon.

    “Daniel legit just said ‘Britt and I are dating,’ and then they did this adorable side hug,” commented a fan who attended the Q&A.

    On February 14, Durant made their relationship Instagram official by posting a photo of them together. The caption reads “My Valentine. Happy Valentine’s Day!” There’s no denying their chemistry now!

    Fans Have Been Shipping Them Since DWTS Season 31

    From the very beginning of the show, Durant and Stewart’s chemistry was undeniable. Their rehearsals were full of laughter and playful banter, and their on-stage performances were electric. Fans took notice of their undeniable connection and began shipping them immediately.

    Fans have taken to social media to express their love for the pair, with some using the hashtag “#Dritt,” to show their support. Some fans have even gone as far as to create fan art and fan fiction depicting Durant and Stewart as a couple, adding fuel to the shipping fire. Within the comments of Durant’s post, various DWTS celebrities and pros are sharing that they weren’t surprised with their announcement.

    As a deaf dancer, Durant brings a unique perspective to the show, and his partnership with Stewart, who is the first Black female pro on the show, has been a shining example of the diversity that the show has been striving to promote in recent years.

    Having been twice honored as People Magazine’s “Sexiest Man Alive” in 1995 and 2000, the actor Brad Pitt has undergone numerous transformations in his appearance. However, an intriguing discovery made by a Twitter user sheds light on something peculiar about his style evolution. Like a chameleon, Pitt has adapted his looks over the years to align with each new woman he has been romantically involved with.

    Shalene McCall

    During Brad Pitt’s appearance in four episodes of Dallas in 1987, the actor, who was 24 years old at the time, had a controversial relationship with his co-star, Shalane McCall. It is said that McCall became Pitt’s first onscreen kiss during their time together on the show. However, once Pitt departed from the series, their relationship did not progress further.

    Christina Applegate

    The actress Christina Applegate and Brad initiated their relationship in 1989 when she was 17 years old, and he was 25. During that period, both of them were striving to establish themselves and achieve recognition in Hollywood. Brad and Christina’s relationship was relatively short-lived. It came to an end when Christina abruptly ended things with Brad in favor of someone else.

    Juliette Lewis

    PHOTOlink/Everett Collection/East News

    At the age of 27, Brad Pitt had yet to achieve significant success in Hollywood when he embarked on a high-profile romance with his 17-year-old co-star from Too Young to Die? Juliette Lewis. Their relationship lasted from late 1989 until 1993, and even years later, Lewis spoke fondly of their time together. In a 2010 interview, she described it as a wonderful and loving romance with a fun and intelligent person. Eventually, when it came to an end, Pitt went on to attain immense fame.

    Jitka Pohledek

    During the premiere of Legends of the Fall in 1994, Pitt attended with model Jitka Pohlodek as his date. Although neither of them has openly discussed their short-lived romance, Pohlodek did provide a brief insight into their connection. In a 2000 interview, she simply stated, “The man glows,” referring to Pitt.

    Gwyneth Paltrow

    Pitt and Paltrow were romantically involved from 1994 to 1997 after meeting on the set of the thriller Seven. Their relationship garnered significant public attention, with Pitt proposing to Paltrow in 1996. However, seven months after the engagement, they decided to call it off.

    Later Paltrow revealed that she genuinely fell in love with him, describing him as incredibly attractive and kind-hearted. But being only 22 years old at the time they met, Paltrow admitted that she was not prepared for such a commitment.

    Claire Forlani

    Following his split from Paltrow, Pitt was rumoured to move on with his Meet Joe Black costar Claire Forlani in 1998, when they walked the red carpet together. Their appearance sparked widespread speculation about a possible romantic relationship between them.

    Jennifer Aniston

    Mary Evans/Allstar/Graham Whitby Boot./EAST NEWS, Hahn Lionel/ABACA/EAST NEWS

    Aniston and Pitt, often referred to as Hollywood’s golden couple, initially met on a blind date arranged by their agents in 1998. Their instant connection propelled them to become one of the most prominent celebrity couples of that era. However, in January 2005, after five years of marriage, they officially announced their separation, leaving fans to consider them as the ones that got away from each other. Despite the end of their romantic relationship, they have managed to rebuild their friendship over time.

    Angelina Jolie

    i-Images, PacificCoastNews.com/EAST NEWS, Adhemar Sburlati/Broadimage/EAST NEWS

    Due to their turbulent beginnings, it was widely believed that Jolie was undoubtedly the love of Pitt’s life. Following his separation from Aniston in 2005, Pitt and Jolie became inseparable and went on to raise six children together. Their shared history made their subsequent divorce all the more surprising, with speculations about the reasons behind their split varying from Jolie disagreeing with Pitt’s parenting methods to rumors of his alleged involvement with his Allied co-star, Marion Cotillard.

    Brad Pitt and George Clooney’s bromance is the talk of Hollywood, filled with playful rivalry, pranks, and mutual respect. From sending fake gifts to public teasing, their friendship has become an iconic symbol of camaraderie in the industry.

    During the filming of “Interview with the Vampire,” Brad Pitt felt so miserable that he called the producer to quit, only to be told it would cost him $40 million. Despite the dark and uncomfortable experience, he decided to stay, contributing to the film’s success.

    In the film “Bullet Train,” Brad Pitt’s connection to “The Lost City” is subtly hinted at through a cameo of Channing Tatum reading a sentimental novel. This clever nod adds a layer of intrigue for fans, showcasing the interconnectedness of the cinematic world.

    Brad Pitt indeed is not afraid to try some traditionally considered ’feminine’ fashion choices on him. Last year he appeared on the red carpet wearing a skirt to a red carpet event, and the fans were absolutely amazed by his style.

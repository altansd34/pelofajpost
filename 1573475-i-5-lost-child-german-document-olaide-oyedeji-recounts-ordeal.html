    Nollywood actress and content creator Olaide Oyedeji has been through a lot in the number of years she has been on earth.

    The mother of four recounted some of her challenges with Biola Bayo on her podcast, Talk to B.

    Legit.ng earlier reported that Oyedeji explained why she underwent body enhancement.

    The actress said she was body shamed because of her shape and the trolling continued after she had her body fixed.

    Narrating her ordeal, the content creator explained that she lost her first daughter at an early age. She said the girl was born premature and had an infection.

    She also addressed claims by a blogger that her substance intake was what affected her baby and eventually led to her death.

    Oyedeji debunked the allegations and explained that she was pained to hear such because of what she went through to take care of her baby.

    The woman, who is known for flaunting her body online, also stated that it was her deceased daughter who gave her her German documents and made her stay aboard easy.

    Olaide Oyedeji says she had five CS

    Speaking further, Olaide said that she had five surgical operations while having her children.

    The actress lamented that her first child didn't live long and she didn't enjoy life. She also revealed a tube was passed through her tummy to feed her.

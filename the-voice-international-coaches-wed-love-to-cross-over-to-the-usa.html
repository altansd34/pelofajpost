    Apart from the talented artists, it’s no secret that The Voice fans are obsessed with loving the series coaches. Fans typically look forward to the coaches’ banter, persuasion styles and their love for the craft of music. Even though The Voice USA has recruited amazing coaches over the years, we’re curious how other celebrities would do in the big red chair.

    The Voice International Coaches We Want on The Voice USA

    1. Will.i.am

    Will.i.am is one of the recurrent coaches on The Voice UK, and it’s totally understandable why. The American rapper and singer has the talent to engage with the audience and calmly compete against other coaches. Hence, if he graced The Voice USA as a coach, we won’t be complaining about it, at all.

    2. Jessie J.

    Jessie J certainly has had her experience on these shows, since she’s coached on The Voice UK, The Voice Kids UK, and The Voice Australia. She has proven to be a dependable coach on each of the shows. Her long years of experience as a vocal mentor will definitely be a great addition to the coaching panel of The Voice USA. Furthermore, her enthusiasm and game attitude every round of the competition are a welcomed element on the show.

    3. Rita Ora

    As the winning coach of The Voice Australia season 11, Rita Ora certainly possesses the capability to breed singing champions. Ora also won The Voice Australia’s spin-off show Generations with father-daughter duo Caitlin and Tim. If she will be offered a seat in the panel, it will not be a shock. She would be a force to reckoned with alongside the other coaches.

    4. Meghan Trainor

    During The Voice UK 2020, fans saw how bubbly Meghan Trainor could be as a coach. People are obviously not unaware of the fact that Trainor is willing to share almost everything to the public. As she possesses such carefree attitude, having her as a coach in The Voice USA will surely be fun to watch.

    5. Keith Urban

    Global hitmaker Keith Urban has been one of the four vocal coaches of The Voice Australia for quite some time. In 2022, Urban announced that he decided to decline the job as he wanted to prioritize his family. However, should he choose to come back as a The Voice judge, seeing him in the USA franchise would be a great option for him.

    6. Lea Salonga

    Multiple award-winning singer Lea Salonga has been a staple coach in The Voice Philippines. Filipino contenders of all ages have witnessed her have grown fond of her as a mentor, and it’s not hard to see why. With her powerful voice and perfect pitch, Salonga is a worthy coach to consider for The Voice USA.

    7. APL.DE.AP

    Filipino-American rapper APL.DE.AP has served as a coach in The Voice Philippines and The Voice Teens Philippines. His international experience and laid-back style of coaching have been a joy to watch ever since. Hence, if he will join the panel of The Voice USA, fans can expect to see his competitive spirit and chill energy at the same time.

    8. Guy Sebastian

    It’s self-explanatory why we want Guy Sebastian to cross over to The Voice USA. With his soulful voice and years of experience in the music industry, Sebastian has imparted quite a chunk of knowledge to his team members in The Voice Australia back in 2020. His presence will surely be welcomed by fans here in America.

    9. Sir Tom Jones

    To have the legendary Sir Tom Jones in The Voice USA will be entertaining and thrilling altogether. His calm confidence will certainly be a likeable element in the show. Also, as a well-respected veteran artist in the industry, we’re curious on how others in the coaching panel will interact with him.

    10. Kelly Rowland

    Pop singer Kelly Rowland was the mastermind behind the best-selling girl group Little Mix. This alone can prove how much Rowland can give as a coach. She’s confirmed this further in The Voice Australia; imagine what magic she could do if she’ll serve as coach in The Voice USA.

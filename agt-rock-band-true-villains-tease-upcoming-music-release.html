    Electrifying rock band, True Villains, who rocked their way into everyone’s hearts during Season 18 of America’s Got Talent, is back in action and gearing up to unleash new music upon their eager fanbase. The Nashville-based quintet, known for their hard-hitting sound and powerful performances, recently sent waves of excitement through social media with a sneak peek into their recording studio sessions.

    True Villains is Recording New Music

    Fans are buzzing with anticipation for what’s to come after True Villains shared an inside look at their new music. The images shared on their Instagram account, captured within the recording studio, show the band fully immersed in the creative process. Although the short videos’ sounds have been muted, the caption’s promise of a teaser has fans eagerly awaiting what could be the next hit from this rock powerhouse.

    “‘Dead To Me’ is coming along nicely! There’s a teaser at the end,” the band wrote.

    Hailing from Music City itself, True Villains comprises frontman Beau Lastavich on vocals, Tim Venerosa on guitar, Greg Herndon on keys, Barry Conaway on bass, and Matt Carter on drums. The band’s journey on AGT shows their unique ability to infuse modern rock with the swagger of classic heavyweights from past decades.

    True Villains’ ability to capture the essence of rock’s golden eras while infusing it with modern energy has garnered them a dedicated fanbase. Their passion for creating impactful music that resonates with listeners is evident in every note they play.

    After their electrifying journey on AGT, True Villains wasted no time diving headfirst into their next creative endeavor. The band recently shared their exciting progress, revealing that they are in the process of recording new music with none other than the renowned producer Nick Raskulinecz, who produced for bands like Foo Fighters, Rush, and Korn.

    Promising a fresh batch of songs that capture their signature sound, the Nashville rock band will once again captivate audiences with their powerful music. As anticipation builds for the release of their new music, fans can expect nothing less than an explosive and electrifying sonic experience from True Villains.

    Their original song “Dead to Me,” was performed live by the band during the show’s Semi-Finals and earned them a standing ovation from the judges. Though they didn’t make it to the final round, True Villains left an unforgettable performance on the AGT stage.

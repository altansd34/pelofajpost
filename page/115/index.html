YouGov Friendship Study Part Three: Britons on making friends

21 Aug 2024, 00:00

Half of Britons (51%) say they find it difficult to make new friends, the YouGov Friendship Study can reveal. A third (35%) find making friends ‘fairly difficult’ and 16% say it’s ‘very difficult' for them, while 8% of Britons find it very easy to make friends and a further third (36%) say it’s fairly easy. The older people are, the easier they find it to make new friends: 38% of the youngest Britons (16-24 year olds) say it’s easy for them to make friends, compared to 47% of those aged 60 or older.

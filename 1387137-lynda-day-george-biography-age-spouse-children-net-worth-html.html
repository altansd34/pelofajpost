    Lynda Day George spouse and children

    The actress was initially married to Joseph Pantano from 1963 to 1970. After this marriage, she got married to Christopher George.

    Lynda and George first met after starring together in the 1966 independent film known as The Gentle Rain. The duo worked together in the 1970 film Chisum, which marked the beginning of their relationship.

    The lovebirds exchanged their wedding vows on 15th May 1970. They lived happily until the death of George, which occurred on 28th November 1983.

    Recently, the public was shocked to learn that Jason Momoa and his wife, Lisa Bonet, split. They seemed to be one the strongest couples in Hollywood with a romantic story that deserved to be the plot of a movie. As a kid, Jason dreamed of Lisa becoming “his” one day, and thanks to fate, that dream came true at the very right moment.

    We at Bright Side want to recall the development of the romantic relationship of one of the most interesting couples in Hollywood.

    Lisa Bonet was Jason Momoa’s crush when he was a kid.

    The beginning of this love story seemed to be planned long before the 2 actually met. The first time Jason saw Lisa was on screen in a popular TV series from the 1980s, and he liked her immediately. At that time, he was only 8 years old and she was 21, but it never stood in the way of this young boy’s childhood crush.

    As Jason himself said when he saw Lisa for the first time, he thought, “I’m going to stalk you for the rest of my life and I’m going to get you.” Actually, he just wanted to meet with her, and this happened more than 20 years later.

    They first met each other in 2005.

    The fateful acquaintance took place at a party with mutual friends in 2005. As Jason said, “We just happened to be in the right place at the right time.” At this party, Lisa agreed to Jason’s invitation to grab a drink with him at a nearby bar. Since that moment, the stars have not parted.

    As Lisa noticed, “Jason basically picked me up and threw me over his shoulder, caveman style!”

    Their age difference was never a problem.

    Jason and Lisa are considered one of the most interesting-looking couples in Hollywood because of their differences in age and height. Although Lisa is 12 years older, Jason wins in terms of height — he towers over the actress by a foot and 2 inches. However, such differences have never been a problem for this couple. It really seemed that they were the kind of people who would stay together forever.

    The couple welcomed their children in 2007 and 2008.

    In 2007, the couple welcomed their first child, Lola, and a year later, she got a brother, named Nakoa-Wolfe. Except for the 2 kids with the Aquaman actor, Lisa also has a daughter from her first marriage, Zoë, who was always supported by Jason. Moreover, Jason has a good relationship with Lisa’s ex-husband — they even call themselves “brothers.” Their family served as a perfect example of harmony in family relationships.

    In 2017, the couple secretly got married.

    Lisa and Jason got married after 12 years of being in a relationship. The ceremony was secret and the media still doesn’t know the details of the wedding, there aren’t even any photos available to the public to this day. What we do know is that it was a small family ceremony that took place in their house in California.

    The couple announced their split in 2022.

    Their family seemed to be so strong, which is why the public was surprised when the couple announced their split recently. On January 13, Jason left a post on his Instagram to explain that they were breaking up. There are no clear reasons as to why the couple has made this decision, but Jason said that love between them carries on. Although we are sad to know that this beautiful union has crumbled, we’re still happy to learn that they are on good terms with each other.

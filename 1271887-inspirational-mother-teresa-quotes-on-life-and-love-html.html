    Mother Teresa quotes are sayings that we should all live by. The nun spent her life in dedication and service to others. Through her numerous good works and speeches, she left us with great and inspiring words that resonate with us and guide us through our lives. Quotes from Mother Teresa teach us how to be better people.

    The missionary and nun was born on 26th August 1910 and till her death in 1997, spent her life helping the needy and sick and encouraging the masses to be more involved in charity work. Years after her death, in September 2016, Mary Teresa Bojaxhiu was honoured by the Catholic Church and was recognized as a saint. She is known as Saint Teresa of Calcutta.

    So, which are the quotations Mother Teresa gave that moved the masses so much?

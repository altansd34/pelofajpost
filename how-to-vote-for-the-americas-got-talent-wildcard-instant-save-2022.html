    The moment has officially come for America’s Got Talent fans. After five weeks of outstanding live performances, fans are able to vote one judge picked contestant back into the competition. Act quick! Wildcard voting will only be open during the AGT results show on Wednesday, September 6, 2022.

    America’s Got Talent fans are voting one act into the finale this week during Wednesday night’s results show. The four judges have officially chosen their redemption seeking contestants, so here’s how to vote.

    How to Vote For Your Favorite AGT Wildcard Contestant

    The official judge Wildcard picks have officially been announced. Simon Cowell picked The Players Choir, Heidi Klum picked Lily Meola, Howie Mandel picked Don McMillan, and Sofia Vergara picked Celia Muñoz. One of these four contestants will return to the stage on Tuesday, September 13 to battle it out for the grand prize of $1 million and a show on the Las Vegas strip.

    Voting opens on Wednesday, September 7 at 8 p.m. ET and promptly closes at 9 p.m. ET. Don’t wait to pick your choice! No matter which time zone you’re in, voting will be open during the East Coast broadcast. As always, fans are able to vote for their favorite acts on the NBC website or through the AGT app. If this vote follows suit to other weeks, fans will be able to vote up to 10 times for their favorite Wildcard Contestant.

    Voting will officially close at the end of the America’s Got Talent Live Show 5 Results Show. It is unknown whether the Wildcard announcement will be made at the end of the episode or at the beginning of the Finale.

    This is the second Wildcard vote of the season. Comedian Jordan Conley was decide upon earlier this season, ahead of the Qualifiers Round. Ahead of Wednesday Night’s show, Metaphysic, Mike E. Winfield, Sara James, Nicolas Ribs, Chapel Hart, Yu Hojin, Drake Milligan Band, and Avery Dixon were announced as the contestants going into the finale. Fans cannot wait to see which three contestants secure the last spots in the season 17 finale.

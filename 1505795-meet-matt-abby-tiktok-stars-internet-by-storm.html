    Why were Matt and Abby cancelled?

    In episode 7 of their podcast, they recounted their experiences of facing online cancellations twice, first in 2021 and then in 2022. The initial incident occurred when they relocated to Hawaii and launched merchandise featuring the phrase "aloha dudes."

    Matt and Abby experiment with a temporary sleep divorce

    Abby and her husband shared a TikTok video where they discussed their experience with a temporary sleep divorce. In the video, Abby explained the reason behind their decision, with her occupying the master bedroom while Matt stayed in the guest bedroom.

    Abby also mentioned that her deep sleeping patterns caused her anxiety at night, as she needed to remain alert for the baby monitor, resulting in minimal sleep. She emphasized that this arrangement was temporary, with a particular focus on ensuring she obtained quality rest during her pregnancy.
